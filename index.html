About Us

Welcome to the Robotics Club, VIT Chennai-a place where innovation meets passion for technology! Our club is dedicated to fostering curiosity and creativity among students interested in robotics and automation. From designing complex robots to understanding the basics of mechanics, programming, and electronics, we aim to nurture future tech leaders.

With a collaborative environment and hands-on learning opportunities, we encourage students to explore, build, and experiment. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn or a seasoned enthusiast looking to solve real-world problems, the Robotics Club offers a platform for everyone to grow and excel. Join us and be a part of an exciting journey into the future of robotics!
